Description

This is a recipe for a lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Beef 500g
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook diced onions until soft (about 5-10 minutes). Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add ground beef and Italian sausage. Cook until fully browned, breaking the meat into small crumbles. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce, tomato paste, and a dash of Worcestershire sauce. Let it simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes.
